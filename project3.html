Cryfty : Web , Desktop & Mobile applicationsCryfty

JavaFX, Symfony, CodeNameOne

Cryfty is a platform emulating an NFT marketplace and a cryptocurrency trading & mining functionalities. You will find the different source codes for the 3 applications on the repos : CryftyWeb , CryftyJava & CryftyMobile. The technologies used were primarily but not entirely : Symfony , JavaFX and Java's CodeName One. .
